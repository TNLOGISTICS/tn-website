About Us

Freight forwarding services covering ocean, air, customs, and project cargo from Korea.

TN Logistics Co., Ltd. was established on April 22, 2016. We provide ocean freight, air freight, customs clearance coordination, and project cargo services. With offices in Seoul and Busan, we support global trade with professionalism and stability.
